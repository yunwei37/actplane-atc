# ACM SIGOPS ATC 2026 — Submission Requirements

**Deadline:** June 10, 2026

## Page Limits

- **Full (long) papers:** 12 pages max
- **Short papers:** 6 pages max
- Page limit **excludes** references and appendices
- Page limit **includes** all text, figures, tables, and footnotes

## Format

- ACM SIGPLAN template: `\documentclass[sigplan,10pt]{acmart}`
- Two-column layout, 10pt font, 12pt single-spaced leading
- Text block: 178 x 229 mm (7 x 9 in), 8mm inter-column spacing
- PDF submission only
- Pages must be numbered; references must be hyperlinked

## Anonymity (Double-Blind)

- Use paper ID instead of author names
- Refer to own prior work in third person
- Anonymize project/system names if publicly associated with authors
- Papers not properly anonymized may be desk-rejected

## Additional Requirements

- **2-page extended abstract** submitted alongside the full paper (same format, reviewed but not published)
- Figures/tables must be legible without magnification
- Color encouraged but must be grayscale-compatible
- Appendices are optional (algorithm details, proofs, code), do not count toward page limit
- Generative AI attestation required (no sections entirely generated by AI)

## Note

ATC transitioned from USENIX to ACM SIGOPS starting in 2026. The template changed from USENIX's own to the ACM `acmart` SIGPLAN variant.
